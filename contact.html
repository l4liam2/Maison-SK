Frequently asked questions

Do you handle permits?

Yes. From pulling permits, to finding the right architect, engineer, and interior designer — we manage the process from start to finish.

Can you work with our architect or designer?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with architects and interior designers — like Cave Studio on our Avenue Laval bathroom — and can also bring in trusted professionals if you need them.

Are you licensed and bonded?

We are an RBQ-licensed general contracting company (RBQ license: 5831-9435-01), bonded through the CESGM.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Montreal, Quebec, and work throughout the greater Montreal area.

What kinds of projects do you take on?

Kitchens, bathrooms, full home renovations, exterior work, and custom projects — from coach-house conversions to complete transformations of century homes.

Fill out the form above with a brief description of your project and we’ll reach out to you to schedule a walk-through.
